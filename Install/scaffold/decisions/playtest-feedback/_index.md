## Observation Types

- **Confusion** — Tester doesn't understand what to do or what happened.
- **Frustration** — Tester knows what to do but can't do it.
- **Delight** — Tester reacts positively.
- **Suggestion** — Tester proposes an idea or feature.
- **Bug** — Tester encounters unexpected or broken behavior.
